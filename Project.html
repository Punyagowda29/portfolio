COURIER MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The "Courier Management System" aims to streamline and optimize the operations of courier services. At its core, the system is designed to enhance customer experience by offering features such as user registration and login, allowing for personalized tracking of parcels. It provides real-time updates on the status of couriers, integrates a pricing calculator based on parameters like weight and distance, and ensures efficient dispatch and delivery management. Online payment integrations further simplify transactions for users. Behind the scenes, users are segmented into roles: the Admin, who oversees the entire system, Employees who manage daily operations, and the Customers who use the system for their courier needs.

IMPLMENTATION OF PAGE REPLACEMENT ALGORITHM USING OPENGL

Visualizing page replacement algorithms using OpenGL involves rendering the operations of algorithms like FIFO, LRU, and OPT graphically. Initialize an OpenGL window and represent memory as a grid or slots. Each page can be shown as a labeled box, with colors indicating hits or faults. Through animations, show pages entering or leaving memory based on the chosen algorithm. Users can interact by selecting page request sequences and algorithms, offering real-time insights into algorithm performance. This approach provides a hands-on understanding, making complex operations visually intuitive.
